About Me

Hey there, welcome to my personal corner of the web! Take a look around and get to know me better.

Here, I hope to develop my technical and personal skills to create impactful products that address important challenges, especially in sustainability.

Aside from programming, I also love to cook, go on bike rides, travel, play board games, and practice aerial arts!
